Introduction to Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, mobile devices, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks are often intended to access, change, or destroy sensitive information, steal money, or interrupt normal business operations.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks. It helps prevent data theft, fraud, and system damage.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing Emails
  • Malware & Viruses
  • Ransomware
  • Identity Theft
  • Data Breaches

How to Stay Safe Online

  • Use strong and unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Do not click suspicious links
  • Keep software updated
  • Use antivirus software
