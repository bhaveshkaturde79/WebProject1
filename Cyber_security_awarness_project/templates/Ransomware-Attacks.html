Ransomware Attacks Awareness

Don't Let Hackers Lock Your Data

What is Ransomware?

Ransomware is a type of malicious software that encrypts your files or locks your system and demands payment (ransom) to restore access.

How Ransomware Works

  • User clicks a malicious link or attachment
  • Malware installs silently
  • Files get encrypted
  • Victim receives ransom demand message

Types of Ransomware

1. Crypto Ransomware

Encrypts personal files like documents, photos, and videos.

2. Locker Ransomware

Locks the entire system so users cannot access their device.

3. Double Extortion

Attackers steal data and threaten to publish it if ransom is not paid.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Back up your data regularly
  • Do not open suspicious emails or attachments
  • Keep your software and OS updated
  • Use strong passwords and 2FA
  • Install trusted antivirus software
