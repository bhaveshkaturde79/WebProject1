👥 Verify Friend Requests

Stay Safe on Social Media by Confirming Who You Connect With

1️⃣ Why Verifying Friend Requests is Important

Accepting friend requests from strangers can expose your personal information, photos, and online activity to scammers, hackers, or fake profiles. Always verify the identity before accepting requests.

2️⃣ How to Verify Friend Requests

  • ✔ Check if you know the person personally.
  • ✔ Look for mutual friends and connections.
  • ✔ Review the profile for suspicious activity or missing details.
  • ✔ Do not accept requests from fake or incomplete profiles.
  • ✔ Report or block suspicious accounts.

3️⃣ Tips for Safe Social Media Connections

  • ✔ Only connect with people you know personally.
  • ✔ Review friend requests before accepting.
  • ✔ Avoid sharing sensitive information online.
  • ✔ Enable privacy settings to control who can send requests.
  • ✔ Report suspicious accounts immediately.
