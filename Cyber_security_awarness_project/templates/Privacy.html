1️⃣ What Are Privacy Settings?

Privacy settings are options in apps, websites, and social media platforms that allow you to control who can see your personal information, posts, and activities.

2️⃣ Why You Should Enable Privacy Settings

Enabling privacy settings helps you:

  • ✔ Control who sees your posts
  • ✔ Protect personal information from strangers
  • ✔ Reduce risk of identity theft
  • ✔ Avoid targeted scams and ads
  • ✔ Secure sensitive account details

3️⃣ Tips for Setting Up Privacy

  • ✔ Limit public visibility of your posts and profile
  • ✔ Turn off location sharing
  • ✔ Review app permissions regularly
  • ✔ Use strong passwords and 2FA
  • ✔ Be careful what you share online
